The Problem

Pharmaceutical Wholesale Still Runs on Phone Calls & Spreadsheets

The industry relies on manual processes that create inefficiencies, delays, and lost revenue across the entire supply chain.

📞

Manual Procurement

Pharmacies call multiple stockists to check medicine availability, wasting hours on phone calls and messages.

📋

Fragmented Records

Orders placed through WhatsApp, phone calls, or handwritten records are impossible to track and audit.

⚠️

Stock Visibility Gaps

Stock information becomes outdated quickly, causing critical medicines to go out of stock without warning.

💰

Lost Revenue

Delayed patient care, excess inventory, procurement errors, and poor supplier relationships cost millions.

Core Platform

Built for the Complexity of Pharmaceutical Logistics

Every feature is designed to eliminate friction in the medicine procurement workflow — from discovery to delivery.

🧭

Intelligent Order Routing

When a pharmacy places an order, the system automatically checks supplier inventory, identifies available stockists, and routes orders intelligently based on availability and business rules.

📊

Real-Time Inventory

Stockists update inventory levels instantly. Pharmacies always see the latest stock information with low-stock notifications and synchronized data across operations.

🛒

Bulk Ordering System

Designed specifically for wholesale procurement with multi-product carts, repeat order templates, saved procurement lists, and fast reorder workflows.

🌐

Supplier Discovery

Search medicines, discover nearby or preferred suppliers, compare availability, and build long-term supplier relationships from one dashboard.

🔒

Secure Transaction Ledger

Every transaction is recorded securely — order history, purchase invoices, delivery records, payment references, and full audit logs.

📈

Procurement Analytics

Gain insights into top-selling medicines, supplier performance, procurement trends, inventory turnover, and revenue opportunities.

Simple Workflow

From Search to Delivery in Four Steps

MedicOrder replaces fragmented manual processes with a seamless digital workflow that keeps every stakeholder connected.

1

Search & Discover

Pharmacies search for medicines and instantly discover available stock across the connected supplier network.

2

Place Bulk Orders
